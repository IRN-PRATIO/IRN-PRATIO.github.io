        • Special attention was paid for a gender balance, with 13 males and 11 females. The foreign partners have strong expertise in the taxonomy, diversity and ecology of fossil and living polycystine radiolarians, including development of innovative techniques in silica biomineralisation. They also develop non destructive analytical methods for the study of radiolarian skeletons and applications of Artificial Intelligence. In terms of taxonomy, French participants have complementary expertise from older time periods and living radiolarians from the Mediterranean Sea. They have a strong expertise in molecular biology, symbiosis and participation of polycystine radiolarians to global biogeochemical cycles and silica/carbon budget.
