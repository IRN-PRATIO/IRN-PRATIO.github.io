      Institutional funding possibilities

      University of Lille

    • Invited Lecturers : Twice a year, ULille launches a call for invited Professors/lecturers that may allow to invite foreign colleagues for one or several weeks;
    • MOBLILEX Scholarships for Master and PhD students, both incoming and outgoing.
    • Collège Doctoral of ULille offer grants to encourage short stay mobility abroad. Ecole Doctorale SMRE co-funds participation to international conferences/workshops.
    • Mobility grants (3 500 €) are offered by the « Initiative d’Excellence » of ULille to PALEO and EVOBIO Master students of the Graduate Programme «Science for a Changing Planet».

        • Université de Bretagne Occidentale

      • Mobility Grants for Master’s students and PhD students (incoming and outgoing) through both UBO and ISblue (grant amounts are variable depending on country of origin)
      • Internship fellowship awarded to a researcher that would like to host a student in their group within the ISblue community (e.g. UBO; ~3 500€)
      • Workshop, Seminars, and Thematic Schools (must be organised by an applicant from one of the ISblue laboratories (~5-10 k€)

          • Sorbonne University

        • Master, PhD grants from the “Institut de l’Ocean”
        • Hosting facilities at Station biologique de Roscoff
